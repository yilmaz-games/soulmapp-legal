Privacy Policy — SoulMapp

Effective date: October 13 2025

1. Overview

Soulmapp (“the App”) is developed and operated by yilmaz.games.
Our goal is to help users reflect, journal, and maintain healthy routines while protecting their privacy.

We collect only the minimal information needed to provide secure authentication, store your personal notes, and send optional reminders.

2. Information We Collect

Type Details Why
Account data Email address and authentication ID from Google or Apple Sign-In To create and secure your account
Journaling entries Text that you voluntarily write in the app To store and sync your personal reflections
Device data (limited) Anonymous technical logs (crash/error reports) To maintain and improve app performance
Notifications Optional local reminders for check-in / check-out times To remind you of your chosen routines

We do not collect advertising identifiers, location data, health data, or analytics profiles.

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide authentication and cloud storage through Supabase (EU-hosted servers).
  • Display and sync your own journal entries.
  • Send local or push notifications if you enable them.
  • Maintain anonymous error logs for stability.

We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely in Supabase databases and is encrypted in transit (TLS).
Access is restricted to authorized yilmaz.games developers for maintenance purposes only.

You can delete your account and all related data at any time by contacting us at info@yilmaz.games.

5. Children’s Privacy

Soulmapp is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

You may: - Request a copy of your stored data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Withdraw consent for notifications or connected services

Send any such request to info@yilmaz.games.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves.
The latest version will always be available at this URL.
